About NeuroSentronics

A disruptive technology platform that is a true game-changer in clinical medicine has arrived. The search for objective, non-invasive, quantitative and real-time measurement of the functions of the Autonomic Nervous System within the brainstem is over. We invite youto confidently unravel the enigma of brainstem autonomic functions objectively using clinically validated measurements that are presented to you in fully calibrated units of clinical functions with absolute zero reference point of functionality relevant in all mammals. Come and acquire the long awaited quantitative, non-invasive and real-time autonomic data you have been hoping to record and analyse using ourNeuroSentronics platform.

If, that is, you are still using the “old” technology of spectral and time-domain analysis of Heart Rate Variability (HRV) to quantify Cardiac Vagal Tone, then now is the time for a change due to the many pitfalls and shortcoming in HRV. Get accurate and true real-time measures of Cardiac Vagal Tone by using our platform.

Let us introduce you to a clinically standardised method of measuring Cardiac Vagal Tone based on a very precise physiological process called “baroreflex”. This vital reflex has a finite response time due to the fixed latency of the neuronal responses and the magnitudes of the responses are continuously adjusted within the brainstem according to the prevailing physiological state of the individual at the time of a particular reflex. Consecutive amplitudes of reflex responses can indicate the stability or varying physiological states of the individual. The standardised atropine derived Linear Vagal Scale (LVS) used for measurement of Cardiac Vagal Tone in our NeuroSentronics platform is clinically validated.
